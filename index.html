About

MWS Notebook was intially created as a personal reference book during my studies to become a Mobile Web Specialist. The site was meant to be a demonstrative of how much I was learning, a way of showcasing newly acquired concepts, technologies and practising in general. Of course with time, the volume and quality of the content became more meaningful, with core fundamental concepts and reusable. Since then, I have worked tirelessly to standardise the content and increase its spectrum, hopefully so that this can be a useful guide to beginners or anyone who need to have a firm understanding i the Fundamentals of becoming a Mobile Web Specialist. The contents of this site has been collaborated to by a handful of educators with careers similar to this who believe that fundamental skills can prepare you for any technology shift as you will have a core understanding of the building blocks of the technology.

So, my name is Matthew Rungwe and welcome to MWS Notebook.
